
## Overview
#### Objective
Understand whether or not augmented reality contributes to poster viewer engagement and information recall. The findings of this work were presented and published at MODE Summit 2023. You can [find the full study here.](http://rave.ohiolink.edu/etdc/view?acc_num=kent1668875945845874)

#### Constraints
At the time of research, many packages for creating AR experiences were geared towards the development and computer science community. Supplementary programming and 3D knowledge had to be aquired to create these tests.
